Risk and Return in Investment

Investing always involves risk, and the potential return is usually proportional to the level of risk taken. High-risk investments, such as stocks and cryptocurrencies, can yield significant returns but also have the potential for substantial losses. Low-risk investments, like government bonds and savings accounts, offer lower returns but greater security.
